Documentation

A stable platform lets product teams spend more time improving service quality and less time recreating operating procedures.

Cloud Platform Research Group

Open infrastructure works best when it feels predictable, observable and ready for ordinary teams to operate.

Director of Web Engineering

Quotes come in two forms. A plain <blockquote> is styled at the element level with a left border and an optional <cite>. The ow-pull-quote family draws large quotation marks for a more prominent, editorial pull quote.

Blockquote

Use a standard <blockquote> with a <p> and a <cite> for an attributed quotation. No classes are required.

A stable platform lets product teams spend more time improving service quality and less time recreating operating procedures.

Cloud Platform Research Group
Markup
<blockquote>
  <p>
    A stable platform lets product teams spend more time improving service
    quality and less time recreating operating procedures.
  </p>
  <cite>Cloud Platform Research Group</cite>
</blockquote>

Pull quote

ow-pull-quote frames the text with quotation marks. Wrap each paragraph in ow-pull-quote-quote and attribute it with ow-pull-quote-citation.

Open infrastructure works best when it feels predictable, observable and ready for ordinary teams to operate.

Director of Web Engineering
Markup
<blockquote class="ow-pull-quote">
  <p class="ow-pull-quote-quote">
    Open infrastructure works best when it feels predictable, observable and
    ready for ordinary teams to operate.
  </p>
  <span class="ow-pull-quote-citation">Director of Web Engineering</span>
</blockquote>

Small and large

ow-pull-quote-small reduces the type and mark size for tighter spaces, while ow-pull-quote-large increases the weight and padding for a hero-scale quote.

The team could move from prototype to production without changing the deployment model.

Director of Web Engineering

The support has been consistently practical, helping us turn complex systems into repeatable everyday operations.

Director of Web Engineering
Markup
<blockquote class="ow-pull-quote-small">
  <p class="ow-pull-quote-quote">
    The team could move from prototype to production without changing the
    deployment model.
  </p>
  <span class="ow-pull-quote-citation">Director of Web Engineering</span>
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="ow-pull-quote-large">
  <p class="ow-pull-quote-quote">
    The support has been consistently practical, helping us turn complex systems
    into repeatable everyday operations.
  </p>
  <span class="ow-pull-quote-citation">Director of Web Engineering</span>
</blockquote>

With an image and multiple paragraphs

Add ow-has-image and an ow-pull-quote-image (for example a company logo) above the quote. Each paragraph is its own ow-pull-quote-quote; the marks wrap the first and last.

The same operational model can run locally, on bare metal and in the cloud.

That consistency makes teams faster because every environment stops feeling like a special case.

CTO, Spicule LTD
Markup
<blockquote class="ow-pull-quote ow-has-image">
  <img class="ow-pull-quote-image" src="/path/to/logo.svg" alt="" />
  <p class="ow-pull-quote-quote">
    The same operational model can run locally, on bare metal and in the cloud.
  </p>
  <p class="ow-pull-quote-quote">
    That consistency makes teams faster because every environment stops feeling
    like a special case.
  </p>
  <span class="ow-pull-quote-citation">CTO, Spicule LTD</span>
</blockquote>
