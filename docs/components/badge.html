Badges display a count or other short numeric value, usually next to a label or heading. They draw attention to a quantity without taking up much space.

Default

The default badge suits neutral counts such as totals or unread items.

24
Markup
<span class="ow-badge">24</span>

Negative

Use the negative badge to highlight counts that need attention, such as errors or failures.

3
Markup
<span class="ow-badge-negative">3</span>

Alongside a label

Pair a badge with text to annotate a count in navigation or headings.

Notifications 12
Markup
<span class="inline-flex items-center gap-2">
  Notifications <span class="ow-badge">12</span>
</span>
